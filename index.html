I am Martin Luthor KIng Jr

I am known for my contributions to the American civil rights movement in the 1960s.

Hello

I was a social activist and Baptist minister, I played a key role in the American civil rights movement from the mid-1950s until my assassination in 1968. I sought equality and human rights for African Americans, the economically disadvantaged and all victims of injustice through peaceful protest.

My "I Have A Dream" speech brought awareness and humanity to the national consciousness about civil rights in America. My stance on political activism in a nonviolent way made me a natural leader and an inspirational figure. I led a social movement of equality during a time when I wasn't supported by most.
